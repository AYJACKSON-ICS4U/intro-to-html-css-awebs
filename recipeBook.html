Pizza Dough

  • Time: 1 hour 6 minutes
  • Serves: 1 pizza
Ingredients Quantity
Warm water 3/4 cup
Active dry yeast 1 envelope
All purpose flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 teaspoon
Salt 3/4 teaspoon
Olive oil 3 tablespoons

Step 1: Pour 3/4 cup warm water into small bowl.
Step 2: Stir in yeast, let disolve for 5 minutes.
Step 3: Brush large bowl with olive oil.
Step 4: Mix 2 cups of flour, sugar and salt in processor.
Step 5: Add yeast mixture and 3 tablespoons of oil and process until a sticky ball.
Step 6: Move to a lightly floured surface and knead dough till smooth.
Step 7: Turn dough in a bowl and coat with oil. Then over with plastic and place in a warm environment until the volume doubles.
Step 8: Punch down and roll out dough.

Beer Stir-Fry

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4 Servings
Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
Beer sirloin 1 pount
Broccoli 1 1/2 cups
Red bell pepers 1
Carrots 2
Green onion 1
Minced galic 1 teaspoon
Soy sauce 2 tablespoon
Sesame seeds 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Heat vegetable oil in a large wok over medium-heat.
Step 2: Cook and stir beef until browned
Step 3: Add broccoli, bell pepers, carrots, green onions and garlic.
Step 4: Cook and stir vegetables for 2 minutes.
Step 5: Season with soy sauce and sesame seeds.
Step 6: Cook and stir until vegetables are tender.

Sloppy Joes

  • Time: 56 minutes
  • Serves: 8 Servings
Ingredients Quantity
Lentils 1 cup
Onions 2
Lean ground beer 1 pound
Green bell pepper 1 large
Ketchup 3/4 cup
Barbeque sauce 3/4 cup
Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon
Minced Garlic 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Place lentils and 2 cups of hot water in a pot and bring to a boil.
Step 2: Simmer lentils until tender and drain.
Step 3: Heat a skillet over medium heat and add onions and cook until softened.
Step 4: Add ground beef and bell peppers and cook/stir until browned.
Step 5: Stir drained lentils, ketchup, barbeque sauce, worcestershire sauce and minced garlic into the skillet.
Step 6: Reduce heat and simmer until the desired consistency
